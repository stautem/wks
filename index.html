Learn about WeKanSkate.

The Western Kansas Skate Project (WeKanSkate) seeks to raise awareness for skateboarders and the need for an updated skatepark in Hays, Kansas.

The WeKanSkate project has also created a committee to work with the city of Hays. The WeKanSkate committee has been working with designers and builders to create a new-updated skatepark.

The Current Skatepark.

The skatepark is located on Grant Avenu, in Aubel Bickle Park Hays, Kansas. It was built in the 90's and hasn't been updated since despite its popularity. The concrete is cracked and chipped and there are sharp pieces of metal that could potentially be harmful.

A new park would draw in people from the surrounding areas. Because Hays is such big hub for travel, updating the skate park will draw in business from people traveling through, as well as people from surrounding towns who have no park.




Our vision for the Future.

WeKanSkate has been working with designers and builders to create a new-updated skatepark in Hays. The proposed rennovations have been aproved in stages by the city of Hays, Kansas. The skatepark will be modernized. Many new obstacles and ramps will be added to the park. Existing obstacles and ramps will be fixed and expanded upon.

The proposed renovations to the skate park will benefit the city of Hays, by providing an attraction unique to the area. In addition, the renovated skate park with provide local youth with a quality form of physical activity. The park would also encourage younger generations to focus on a healthy sport.
